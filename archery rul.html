Archery Rules

Safety First

Always ensure the range is clear before shooting. Never point an arrow at anyone.

Equipment Check

Use only approved bows and arrows. Inspect your equipment before each session.

Shooting Line

Remain behind the shooting line until all arrows have been retrieved.

Scoring

Points are awarded based on where the arrow lands on the target. The center is worth the most points.

Retrieving Arrows

Wait for the signal to retrieve your arrows. Walk, don't run, and be careful not to damage the arrows.

Range Etiquette

Maintain silence during shooting. Be respectful of other archers and their equipment.
