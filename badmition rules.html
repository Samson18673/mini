Badminton Rules

Serving

The serve must be hit below the server's waist. The shuttle must land within the designated service court diagonally opposite.

Scoring

A point is scored every time the shuttlecock lands within the opponent's court or if they commit a fault. A match consists of the best of three games, each game played to 21 points.

Faults

Common faults include hitting the shuttlecock out of bounds, failing to return it before it hits the ground twice, or touching the net.

Court Positioning

Players must stay within their respective sides of the court and not touch the net with their body or racket.

Lets

A "let" is called when there is an unforeseen interruption, such as a shuttlecock from another court landing on the playing area. The point is replayed.

Equipment

Only approved rackets and shuttlecocks should be used. Ensure the net is at the correct height.
