Basketball Rules

Scoring

A successful field goal inside the three-point arc is worth two points, while a field goal beyond the three-point arc is worth three points. A successful free throw is worth one point.

Traveling

Traveling is an illegal movement of one or both feet beyond the limits outlined in the rules, in any direction, while holding a live ball on the court.

Double Dribble

A player cannot dribble the ball, stop, and then dribble again. Also, a player cannot dribble with both hands at the same time.

Fouls

Personal fouls include illegal physical contact such as pushing, holding, or hitting. Technical fouls are for unsportsmanlike conduct.

Three-Second Rule

An offensive player cannot remain in the opponent's painted area (the key) for more than three consecutive seconds.

Backcourt Violation

Once the offensive team has crossed the half-court line, they cannot return the ball to the backcourt.
