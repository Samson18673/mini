Football Rules

Duration of the Game

A standard football match consists of two 45-minute halves, with a 15-minute halftime break.

Offside Rule

A player is in an offside position if they are nearer to the opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-last opponent when the ball is played to them.

Fouls and Misconduct

Fouls are penalized with direct or indirect free kicks. Yellow cards are issued for cautions, and red cards for sending-offs.

Penalty Kicks

A penalty kick is awarded when a player commits a foul inside their own penalty area.

Throw-ins

When the ball goes out of bounds along the touchline, a throw-in is awarded to the opposing team.

Goal Kicks and Corner Kicks

A goal kick is awarded when the ball goes out of bounds over the goal line last touched by an attacking player. A corner kick is awarded when it's last touched by a defending player.

Number of Players

Each team consists of 11 players, including a goalkeeper.
