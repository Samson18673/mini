What is Sports?

Sports are physical activities and games that involve skill, competition, and often physical exertion. They provide entertainment, promote physical fitness, and foster social interaction.

Advantages of Sports

  • Improved physical health and fitness
  • Enhanced mental well-being
  • Development of teamwork and social skills
  • Stress reduction
  • Increased discipline and focus
  • Building character and resilience

Cricket

A bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team.

Badminton

A racquet sport played by either two opposing players (singles) or two opposing pairs (doubles), who take positions on opposite halves of a rectangular court that is divided by a net.

More information: Wikipedia

Football (Soccer)

A team sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. The objective of the game is to score by kicking or heading the ball into the opposing goal.

More information: Wikipedia

Basketball

A team sport in which two teams of five active players each try to score points against one another by throwing a basketball through a 305 cm (10 feet) high hoop under organized rules.

More information: Wikipedia

Esports

Competitive video gaming. Esports often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams.

More information: Wikipedia

Athletics

A collection of sporting events that involve competitive running, jumping, throwing, and walking. The most common types of athletics competitions are track and field, road running, cross country running, and race walking.
